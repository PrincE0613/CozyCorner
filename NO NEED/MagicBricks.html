New Delhi is the Northern Indian state, which is known for its modern and rich infrastructure and contemporary architecture. Along with all the aforementioned attraction New Delhi is a stunning city that is hub of different commercial and residential activities. This city is gradually becoming a major IT & ITES, services industry hub where a lot of people are searching for residential properties for sale or rent in New Delhi. Some of the renowned residential localities in New Delhi are Dwarka, Green Park, Hauz Khas, Pitampura, Laxmi Nagar and more. A huge number of people are shifting in this city for their livelihood and therefore the demand for houses for sale in New Delhi is rising every day. And to meet this demand many renowned builders are coming up with new project in New Delhi. This suburb of Delhi is well connected with rest of the places in Delhi NCR like Gurgaon, Noida, Faridabad, and Ghaziabad.
